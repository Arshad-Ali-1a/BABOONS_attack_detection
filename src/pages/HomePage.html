What we do?

Baboons Shield, our state-of-the-art application, stands as an impenetrable fortress against the relentless tide of zero-day attacks. Leveraging advanced machine learning algorithms, it operates seamlessly with native OS blocking technologies, ensuring real-time detection and swift mitigation of emerging threats. Meticulously filtering DNS traffic, Baboons Shield acts as a vigilant guardian, proactively identifying and neutralizing potential risks before they can infiltrate your network. With 24/7 monitoring, the application provides continuous protection, adapting to the ever-evolving landscape of cyber threats. Its user-friendly interface guarantees easy navigation and management of cybersecurity settings, while the comprehensive defense mechanism offers peace of mind, allowing you to focus on your work with confidence in the fortified security provided by Baboons Shield.
